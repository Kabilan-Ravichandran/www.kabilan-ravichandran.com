An in-depth analysis of coffee shop operations to identify inefficiencies and propose data-driven strategies. U.S. Flight Operations & Delay Analysis

Developed a comprehensive Tableau dashboard to analyze over 26 million U.S. flight records from 2023. The interactive dashboard explores nationwide patterns in flight volume, delays, diversions, and their underlying causes, with filters for drilling down by airline, airport, and date.

Technologies Used: Tableau, SQL, Python (Pandas), Mapbox

A company-sponsored capstone project with Microsoft.

Our team designed a low-cost ESG solution empowering SMEs to track sustainability, meet regulations, and gain insights. Technologies Used: Microsoft Power Platform, Power Apps, Power BI, SharePoint, Power Automate.
